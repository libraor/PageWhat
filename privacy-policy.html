PageWhat - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 13, 2026

Introduction

PageWhat ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our Chrome extension.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data or monitored web content on external servers.

All data processing happens locally on your device:

  • Monitored Content: Web page content is analyzed locally in your browser. No content is sent to any external server.
  • Task Configuration: Your monitoring tasks, URLs, and settings are stored locally using chrome.storage.local.
  • Change History: Detected changes and diff results are stored locally on your device.

Data Usage

All data collected by the extension is used solely for the purpose of monitoring web page changes as configured by you:

  • Checking for content changes on monitored URLs
  • Displaying change history and diff results
  • Sending desktop notifications when changes are detected

Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • alarms - To schedule periodic checks at your configured intervals
  • storage - To save your tasks, settings, and change history locally
  • scripting - To inject content extraction scripts into monitored pages
  • notifications - To send desktop notifications when changes are detected
  • offscreen - To fetch and parse web pages in the background
  • activeTab - To access the current tab when you use the "Monitor This Page" feature
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>) - Required to monitor any URL you specify

Third-Party Services

The extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API, your data is protected by your browser's security mechanisms.

Data Deletion

You can delete all extension data at any time:

  • Individual tasks and their history can be deleted from the Options page
  • All data will be removed when you uninstall the extension from Chrome

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
